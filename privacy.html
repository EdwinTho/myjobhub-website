Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

MyJobHub ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

Information you provide: Job application details, contact information, resume content, and cover letter content that you enter into the app. This data is stored locally on your device and is never sold or shared with third parties.

AI feature usage: When you use AI-powered features (resume improvement, cover letter generation, ATS scanning), the relevant resume or cover letter content is sent to our secure processing server to generate your results. This content is not stored after processing is complete.

Analytics: We collect anonymous usage data (such as which features you use and how often) to help us improve the app. This is handled by PostHog. No personally identifiable information is included.

Crash reports: If the app crashes, an anonymous crash report is sent to Sentry to help us identify and fix bugs. These reports contain technical information about the crash but do not include your resume or application data.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the app's features
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues
  • To understand how users interact with the app

Data Storage

Your job search data (applications, contacts, resumes, cover letters) is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage. We do not have access to this data.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • PostHog — anonymous usage analytics
  • Sentry — crash reporting
  • Anthropic — AI content generation (content is processed and not retained)

Data Retention

Your local data remains on your device until you delete the app. AI-processed content is not retained after your request is fulfilled.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: support@myjobhub.app
